    About.

    Mads, of self-named OCUUDA 3D MODELING, is a 3D artist focused primarily on vTuber & environment modeling. OCUUDA is known for his ability to match and translate two-dimensional artstyles into a three-dimensional space, as shown in his work with the streaming group VchiBan, individual creators such as Pumpkin Potion, and many others.

    Alongside contract work, Mads creates paid and free assets for vTubers & 3D artists alike.
